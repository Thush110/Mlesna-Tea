Our Story

Tea is an art and at Mlesna we have perfected the art of tea. To taste a cup of Mlesna tea is to experience the passion, the dedication and the skilled art that went into creating a world class brand. Zesta gives consumers the best quality cup of Ceylon tea which is why it is from the No. 1 branded tea company in Sri Lanka. Our tea is based on our total commitment to quality, from the moment of handpicking two leaves and bud to being packed fresh at source, within days of picking.

The perfect cup of tea brewed with the world's finest; Ceylon tea is the enchantment that will fill your cup.
