"TROPICAL FRUIT" TEA COCKTAIL

While we recomended mangos, figs, papayas and pineapple for this cocktail, any tropical fruit will work well in this cocktail. To avoid having to add suger use a demi-sec sparkling white wine. If you are using suger, do not use too much as it will be too sweet and therefor not an effective thirst quencher. Remember to serve well-chilled, without ice cube.

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 litre(18 fl oz/ 2 cups)strong chill tea.
  • 2 cl(1 1/2 tablespoons) rum.
  • 2 cl(1 1/2 tablespoons) gin.
  • 250g (9 oz/ 1 cup ) thinly sliced fresh mango, figs, papaya or pineapple.
  • 1 bottle sparkling white wine.
Method

    Prepare a strong brew of tea and allow the brew to cool down to room temparature before chilling. This practise will ensure that the chilled brew will not cloud, provided the tea used is of good quality Once the tea is chilled add the rum and gini. Devide the fruit between four glasses then pour over the mixture of tea, rum and gini. Finally top up with the sparkling wine.

The perfect cup of tea brewed with the world's finest; Ceylon tea is the enchantment that will fill your cup.
